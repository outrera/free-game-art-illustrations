Collection of 94 items made from old works that have entered public domain.

I have searched for works that looked that could be used in games on sites like the Internet Archive, Wikimedia Commons or Public Domain Review. I have then taken the selected works and processed them manually (retouched, colored, cut out, resized, ...) with GIMP.

The collection contains a lot of animal sprites (mainly fish and birds), architectural sprites, nature sprites (trees, rocks, flowers, ...), decorations (probably good for GUIs), black-and-white drawings, masks, character portraits, misc items (skulls, armor, ...) and even some fonts and pixel art (redrawn from stitching pattern book).

Most items are game-ready 2D artworks (sprites, backgrounds etc.). You can also remix or combine them to suit your specific needs, there's no limit to what you can do!

The list of sources is included in the archive.
